Core capabilities

Eight automated phases, one diagnosis.

Based on the reference Ad Diagnosis page: macro health, campaign triage, keyword classification, search-term actions, and generated reports.

Macro diagnosis

Store-level PPC health in one executive view.

  • Core metrics dashboardACOS, ROAS, ad sales, orders, spend, conversion, and health distribution in one consolidated report.
  • Health status tiersClassify campaigns into Star, Healthy, Problematic, and observation states based on thresholds.
  • Spend share by tierSee whether budget is flowing toward winners or being consumed by problematic campaigns.
Campaign diagnosis

Find which campaigns deserve scale, repair, or pause.

  • Per-campaign performanceSpend, sales, ACOS, ROAS, orders, CTR, CVR, budget, and diagnosis status.
  • Action labelsScale Star campaigns, monitor Healthy campaigns, and pause or repair Problematic campaigns.
  • Budget movementIdentify budget-capped winners and campaigns consuming budget without enough return.
Keyword diagnosis

Label every keyword with what should happen next.

  • Keep / Optimize / Observe / PauseClassify each keyword with evidence, not spreadsheet guesswork.
  • Match-type breakdownSee whether exact, phrase, and broad structures are carrying the right work.
  • Per-row recommended actionEach keyword gets a practical instruction for review or execution.
Search-term diagnosis

Harvest winners and draft negative lists.

  • Search-term labelsKeep, optimize, observe, or negative based on spend, orders, ACOS, and ROAS.
  • Auto to exact migrationPromote auto-discovered winners into tighter keyword control with ROAS evidence.
  • Negative list draftsReady-to-copy negative terms with the rationale attached.

Common questions

Before you try it.

How often should I run a diagnosis?

Weekly for actively managed ASINs and monthly for stable SKUs. Short windows move faster but carry more noise.

Can I diagnose multiple ASINs?

Yes. Run per ASIN, then aggregate JSON summaries into a portfolio-level view.

Does it work for Auto campaigns?

Yes. Auto campaigns get search-term analysis and auto-to-exact migration suggestions when terms show strong ROAS.

Can thresholds be customized?

Yes. Health classification can adapt to category, margin profile, ACOS target, and growth stage.
