The problem

By the time you spot the move, the window has closed.

Competitors change your best next action every week — a price drop, a fresh image set, a stacked coupon, a keyword push, a swing in reviews. Manual spot-checks are slow, sporadic, and rarely tied to a decision, so the moment to respond passes before anyone notices.

Spot-checks miss the moment

Eyeballing a few competitor pages once in a while catches the change days late — long after the price drop, new A+, or coupon has already moved buyers.

Signals not tied to action

A screenshot of a rival’s move is not a decision. Without a clear “so what,” observations pile up and nothing changes on your own listings or campaigns.

Openings close before you react

A competitor stockout or a sudden rating dip is a short-lived opening. By the time it surfaces in a manual review, the gap has usually closed again.
