FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the token-based pricing actually work?

Unlike traditional tools that charge a high flat fee or a percentage of ad spend, Luckee charges based on the AI reasoning (tokens) consumed to optimize your account. This means you only pay for the exact value and computations generated. It transparently scales up during heavy optimization phases and down during quiet periods.

Will the AI make changes without my permission?

No. You have complete control. Critical, high-impact changes (like significant bid adjustments, budget expansions, or new keyword launches) are placed in your 'Morning Queue' for 1-click human approval. Low-risk background tasks can be set to run automatically, but strictly within the financial guardrails you define.

Do I need to be an Amazon Ads expert to use this?

Not at all. Luckee acts as your expert. It translates complex data into simple, actionable business decisions associated with your ultimate goals (like Net Margin or TACoS). If you have questions about a recommendation, you can chat with the agent directly in plain English for a detailed explanation.

How is Luckee different from rules-based PPC software?

Rules-based software blindly follows 'if-then' statements (e.g., 'If ACOS > 30%, lower bid'). The Luckee agent utilizes advanced foundational models capable of reasoning. It looks at the holistic picture: organic rank, inventory velocity, recent promotions, and competitor pricing before making a nuanced, profit-focused decision.

Can I connect it to my Slack or Email?

Yes! Luckee is designed to fit seamlessly into your existing workflow. You can receive your daily optimization briefings directly in Slack or via Email, allowing your team to approve or discuss actions without logging into another dashboard.
