The shift

Your listing looks good. But can it talk back?

The old listing playbook optimized for keywords and human scanning. The new shopping path asks AI agents to answer fit, safety, compatibility, contents, and comparison questions. If the page does not say it, the agent cannot recommend it.

Rufus can answer

“Yes, this fits your use case.”

Clear use cases, constraints, included items, compatibility, dimensions, and reason-to-buy evidence are visible in the listing.

→
Rufus cannot answer

“I am not sure.”

Important shopper questions are implied, hidden in images, absent from A+ content, or unsupported by product facts.
